Meet GARY

Generative AI Reviewer for You – Personalized Learning Powered by AI

What is GARY?

GARY is an AI-driven feedback tool that evaluates student responses, offers personalized insights, and generates follow-up questions to deepen understanding and reflection.

How It Works

GARY uses large language models to analyze written answers, score them based on rubrics, and provide adaptive feedback aligned with student needs.

Impact

Piloted in psychology courses, GARY improved student engagement and enabled instructors to focus on high-level support through oversight of generated feedback.
